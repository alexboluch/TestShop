# Test Shop

### In it, you can buy products and view the sales history.
### To buy a product, you need to register a new user and log in.
### Select an item from the list and go to the purchase window.
### Choose the required quantity and the seller.
### The purchased item, quantity, seller and date of purchase will be shown in the sale tab.
### In the sales history tab, the change in prices for goods is recorded.
### The footer at the bottom of the page displays the time and date when the page was rendered.
